5. What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

Both are used to create lists.

UL is unordered lists. Use bullets.

  • Item 1
  • Item 2

OL is ordered lists. Use numbers.

  1. Item 1
  2. Item 2

But we can change numbers, adding attribute. type="A", only OL.

  1. Item 1
  2. Item 2
