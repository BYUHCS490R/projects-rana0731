Top K-Pop Groups Ranking

This page shows a simple ranking of some of the most popular K-pop groups in the world today. The ranking is based on global popularity, music sales, streaming numbers, and overall influence in the industry.

  • 1. BTS

    • One of the most famous K-pop groups worldwide. They break records in album sales, chart rankings, and concerts.

  • 2. BLACKPINK

    • A global girl group known for their powerful performances and massive international fanbase.

  • 3. Seventeen

    • A self-producing group popular for their sharp choreography and strong album sales.

  • 4. Stray Kids

    • A 4th-generation leader with high-energy music and strong global tours.

  • 5. NewJeans

    • A fast-rising girl group known for their trend-setting music and huge digital streaming numbers.

  • 6. TXT (Tomorrow X Together)

    • A popular 4th-generation boy group with a loyal fandom and strong charts.

  • 7. IVE

    • A girl group famous for catchy hits and strong performances on digital charts.

  • 8. Aespa

    • Known for their unique AI-concept, vocals, and strong international following.

  • 9. TWICE

    • A long-running top girl group with millions of fans and record-breaking tours.

  • 10. NCT

    • A multi-unit boy group with a large global fandom and consistent music success.
