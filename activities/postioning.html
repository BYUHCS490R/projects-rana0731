1. What is normal flow in HTML?

Normal Flow is HTML elements are laid out on a page. Block-level elements (like div, p, h1) Inline elements (like span, a, strong)

2. What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

The float property allows an image or a div to be taken out of normal flow and pushed to the left or right.

Temple pic is example of float:right
