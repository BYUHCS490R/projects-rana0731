She is now 56 years old but look ten years younger. I watched her on "Dancing Queen" (2012), big hit Korean movie for the first time. And I really admired her talents, singing, dancing and her acting on that movie. After that I found out she is a singer. And I listened her all the songs, I was like WOW, I really love her. Then, I watched Television Shows which she participated, to know more about her, who she is exactly and her character. I loved more and I felt to become more like her, my role model.
