Top K-Pop Groups Ranking

This page shows a simple ranking of some of the most popular K-pop groups in the world today. The ranking is based on global popularity, music sales, streaming numbers, and overall influence in the industry.

  • 1. BLACKPINK

    • As kpop female idols, BLACKPINK’s charisma and record-breaking achievements, like being the first K-pop group to headline Coachella, keep them at the top.

  • 2. BTS

    • BTS’s global influence, including topping the Billboard charts, makes them a cornerstone of kpop boy bands.

  • 3. IVE

    • IVE’s “chaebol” concept and polished performances make them a favorite among korean girl bands.

  • 4. SEVENTEEN

    • Their unity and hands-on approach to music make them a standout in kpop groups male.

  • 5. TWICE

    • TWICE, a nine-member girl band, continues to captivate with their colorful concepts and catchy hits. Their longevity proves their staying power in kpop generations. A long-running top girl group with millions of fans and record-breaking tours.

  • 6. AESPA

    • Aespa’s futuristic concepts and high-energy tracks have made them a leader among female kpop groups in the fourth generation. Known for their unique AI-concept, vocals, and strong international following.

  • 7. LE SSERAFIM

    • LE SSERAFIM’s ethereal sound and empowering messages in songs like “ANTIFRAGILE” have made them a rising star among korean girl bands. Their harmonious musicality sets them apart in the kpop girl scene.

  • 8. STRAY KIDS

    • Stray Kids’ dynamic performances and self-produced tracks like “God’s Menu” make them a force in kpop boy groups.

  • 9. (G)I-DLE

    • (G)I-DLE’s unique sound and involvement in songwriting, seen in hits like “Queencard,” make them a standout girl group. Their bold themes and diverse musical style elevate their status among kpop female idols.

  • 10. ENHYPEN

    • Formed through I-Land, ENHYPEN’s powerful performances and global appeal have cemented their place among kpop guy groups.
