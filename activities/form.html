The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

direct where to send the form data when the user submits it

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

how the form data is sent to the server, post or get

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

get is visible (forms will be shown on url), less secure
post -> add, update, delete, is hidden change (action "__.html" will be shown on URL) more secure
