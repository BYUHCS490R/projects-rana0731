BLACKPINK

Rose Jennie Jisoo Lisa

The group has four amazing members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Often called the “biggest girl group in the world,” they’ve become a huge part of the Korean Wave and are known for their strong “girl crush” concept in K-pop, celebrating confidence and empowering women everywhere.

Each member of the group shines in her own way. Jisoo represents Dior and Cartier as a global ambassador. Jennie is the face of Chanel, Calvin Klein, and other brands like Jacquemus and Tamburins. Rosé brings her elegance to Yves Saint Laurent, Tiffany & Co., and Rimowa, while Lisa represents top luxury brands such as Celine, Bvlgari, and Louis Vuitton.
